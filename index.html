ESP32 is one of the most popular microcontroller boards for IoT applications. It combines powerful processing capabilities with built-in WiFi and Bluetooth connectivity.

Why Choose ESP32?

  • Dual-core processor running at 240MHz
  • Built-in WiFi and Bluetooth
  • 30 GPIO pins
  • Extensive library and community support
  • Low power consumption
